Analytics Case Study

Flight Data Analysis & Operational Performance Insights

An end-to-end data project focused on delay behavior, cancellations, route inefficiencies, and translating flight data into operational insight.

50,000+ Flight records analyzed
3+ Dashboard views designed
30–40% Higher delay frequency in peak periods
End-to-End Cleaning to reporting workflow

Overview

This project analyzes airline operational data to uncover patterns in flight delays and cancellations. The goal was to simulate a real-world analytics workflow by transforming raw datasets into structured, decision-ready insights through cleaning, querying, visualization, and reporting.

Business Context

Flight disruptions affect operational cost, scheduling efficiency, customer experience, and performance reliability. Understanding where delays concentrate creates a stronger foundation for route optimization and operational planning.

Objective

  • Identify meaningful patterns in delays and cancellations.
  • Analyze operational bottlenecks across routes and time segments.
  • Translate raw airline data into dashboard-ready business insight.

Dataset

  • Processed 50,000+ flight records across multiple variables.
  • Worked with fields including airline, departure timing, delay duration, and cancellation status.
  • Used structured and semi-structured data formats including JSON-based records.

Data Pipeline & Workflow

  • Imported raw flight data and structured it into analysis-ready tables.
  • Handled missing values, duplicate entries, and inconsistent field formatting.
  • Standardized time formats and built derived fields for delay categories and segmented analysis.
  • Prepared datasets for SQL querying, dashboarding, and comparative performance review.

Analysis & Methodology

  • Used SQL to filter, aggregate, and compare performance metrics across airlines and routes.
  • Applied Python with Pandas and NumPy for data cleaning, structuring, and exploratory analysis.
  • Evaluated delay distribution by time windows, routes, and operational segments.

Visualization & Reporting

  • Built 3+ dashboard views in Tableau and Power BI.
  • Designed visuals highlighting delay concentration, cancellation trends, and route performance.
  • Framed insights using clear data storytelling and business-focused reporting logic.

Key Insights

  • Peak travel periods showed significantly higher delay frequency.
  • Certain routes demonstrated consistently elevated delay behavior.
  • Cancellation patterns suggested scheduling and resource-allocation inefficiencies.

Business Impact & Recommendations

  • Supports data-driven scheduling and route optimization decisions.
  • Provides a framework for identifying high-friction operational segments.
  • Recommends deeper investigation of high-delay routes and peak-hour planning strategy.

What This Demonstrates

This project demonstrates the ability to work with large datasets, structure raw information for analysis, extract insights using SQL and Python, and communicate findings through dashboard-style reporting that aligns with real-world business intelligence workflows.
