Overview

This portfolio serves as the central platform for presenting my technical work, analytics projects, development skills, and applied problem-solving ability. It is built not just to list projects, but to explain them in a way that communicates depth, structure, and credibility.

Objective

  • Present projects clearly and professionally.
  • Create a stronger personal brand around analytics and technical work.
  • Provide recruiters with direct access to proof of skill and execution.

Design Strategy

  • Used a clean modern visual style with strong hierarchy and readable spacing.
  • Structured the site around summary first, then proof and case-study depth.
  • Added project cards, preview images, badges, and detailed sections for better navigation.

Key Features

  • Featured work cards with filters and quick project summaries.
  • Dedicated case-study pages for deeper understanding of methods and outcomes.
  • Responsive design for mobile and desktop browsing.
  • Direct access to project repositories and professional materials.

Impact

  • Acts as a centralized proof engine for applications and recruiter review.
  • Strengthens credibility by showing both results and process.
  • Improves how technical work is positioned professionally.

What This Demonstrates

  • Ability to present technical work with clarity and structure.
  • Understanding of user experience, branding, and navigation.
  • Strong connection between technical depth and professional storytelling.
