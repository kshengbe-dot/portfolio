How I Work With Data

I approach data problems using a structured workflow that moves from raw information to insight and decision support.

1. Collect & Prepare Gather raw data, inspect fields, handle missing values, and structure inputs for analysis.
2. Clean & Transform Standardize formats, remove inconsistencies, build derived fields, and prepare usable datasets.
3. Analyze Patterns Use SQL, Python, and exploratory analysis to identify trends, outliers, and performance signals.
4. Visualize Findings Present insights using dashboards, charts, and structured reporting for easier interpretation.
5. Support Decisions Translate technical findings into recommendations and next steps that connect to real-world action.
